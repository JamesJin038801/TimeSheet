How To Use
  1. Before starting, you have to setup clients, projects, and company in setting
  2. Then you can add new time records
  3. You can analyst time records by bar/line chart
  4. You may invoice timesheet and send to client
Timesheet
  1. Click title to change period
  2. Click plus icon to add new time record
  3. Click export/email icon to export/email in html, excel or CSV file
  4. Click filter "s" icon to filter by status
  5. Click filter "c" icon to filter by client
  6. Click filter "p" icon to filter by project
  7. Click header wording to sort data
  8. Click item to update, delete and copy
  9. Long click item to be paid
  10. Fling screen to go previous/next period of data
Invoice
  1. Click plus icon to add new invoice
  2. Click invoice item to open by PDF reader
  3. Long click invoice item to update/delete/email invoice
Add New Invoice
  1. First you have to select client
  2. Then you can pick up timesheet by clicking "Hours", only status in ""open" or "follow up" available
Charts
  1. Shows monthly amount and hours in bar chart and line chart
  2. Click filter "c" icon to filter by client
  3. Click filter "p" icon to filter by project
  4. Fling screen for previous/next year chart
